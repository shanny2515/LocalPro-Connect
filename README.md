## Overview

Local Pro Connect is a web application designed to help people easily locate and book local services online. It streamlines the process of finding service providers in your area and allows you to schedule appointments from the comfort of your home.

---

## Features

- **Service Directory:** Browse a curated list of local services available in your area.
- **Provider Profiles:** View details about each service provider, including their schedule and contact information.
- **Booking System:** Book appointments directly through a user-friendly online form.
- **Schedule Display:** See available times and dates for each provider before booking.

---

## Tech Stack

- **JavaScript** for application logic and interactivity.
- **CSS** for layout and styling (with a focus on responsive, user-friendly design).
- No frameworks or libraries required—simply open in your browser and use.

---

## Usage

1. **Get Started:** Open the website in your browser.
2. **Browse Services:** Click the button to get started and view available services.
3. **View Providers:** Select a service, then press the providers button to see who is available.
4. **Book a Service:** Click the "Book Now" button to access the booking form.
5. **Submit Booking:** Fill out your details and submit the form to reserve your spot.

No installation or configuration is required—just open the site and you’re ready to go.
